To use your detailed shop logo (raster) on the About page:

1. Copy your high-resolution logo image (PNG or JPG) into this folder as `assets/logo-full.png`.
   - Recommended size: at least 800px wide.
   - Transparent PNG preferred for overlay on backgrounds.

2. The site will automatically use `assets/logo-full.png` if present; otherwise it falls back to `assets/logo-stacked.svg`.

3. If you'd like me to produce high-fidelity vector tracing from your raster, reply and I will trace and refine the SVG for a small design pass.
